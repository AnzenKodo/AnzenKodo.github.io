The Most Important Skill To Learn In The Next 10 Years With Devon Eriksen

  • You have to trust yourself that you can do hard things. by making first small steps. You can do it even if you think it is elon musk level hard.
  • Take responsibility for my own life, that doesn't depend on other great human beings (e.g: Elon Musk) to tell me what I need to do.
    • What do I need to learn?
    • What do I need to do?
    • What are my goals?
    • What is important to me?
    • What do i wish to achieve?
  • How can I use my current tools to achieve my goals? If I can't do that with the tools i have, what kind of new tool do I need to create?
  • When you say you are an "artist". You limit yourself in one profession. You have reduced yourself as a tool not a human (generalist).
    • That's why modern people fear AI.
  • Many reasons people don't do the things that they want to do because it threatens their sense of identity.
    • E.g: Someone who identifies as an "artist" doesn't like to do things that they don't consider as an "art". So that's why he fears AI, because AI will take his identity away from him.
    • E.g: I can't do that because that's not a programmer's problem.
    • E.g: It threatens my sense of who i am as a professional.
  • Anarcho capitalism want to replace the government but can't because some part of society still needs government. So that's why we need new tools to replace the government one by one.
    • E.g: We have figured out "how to do money without a government".
    • Next need to figure out how to do criminal justice without government.
  • You never have to one thing to fulfill your life. You can do many things to fulfill your life.
    • Hance you can avoid your old goals.
  • Story makes things real.
    • Elon musk do the things that he doesn't because he's want make stories of sci-fi a reality.
    • Reality was fiction before they were real.
